The Cruise Club - St.John'street
This club is located in the centre of the beautiful city of Chester, Cruise will feature six very distinct themed areas and brings together a hideaway of luxury and style. With pulse quickening sounds from the hottest DJs and beats of some of the most progressive music mixes, old and new, it is destined to become the top night spot for discerning people.

Open until midnight Sunday to Wednesday, 1am Thursday and 2am Friday and Saturday, we are the perfect venue for you to enjoy a superb night out. DJ's play funky beats, euphoric house tunes and classic anthems every night of the week. Revolution Chester will leave you gagging for more!
